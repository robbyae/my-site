A. Gretchaninoff - Après le bal (arr. R. Strong)

This is my guitar arrangement of Aleksandr Gretchaninoff's Après le bal, Op. 98 No. 13 for piano solo. The score was made with Musescore and was very mindfully designed. The font EB Garamond was specifically selected for all text elements because of its simplistic elegance. I carefully arranged the stems and rests, and placed each fingering and staccato marking for maximum readability and visual grace.
