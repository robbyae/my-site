"The Two Worlds of Guitar Notation"

This paper explores the relationship between tablature and staff notation for the modern guitarist. It provides historical context for the widespread use of tablature as the primary notation of Renaissance lute music and its evolution to becoming an easy way for modern guitarists to share pieces with each other online. It also explores the technical and intellectual nuances of tablature and staff notation and each method's place in the modern guitarist's collection of skills.
