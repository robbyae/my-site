Audio Essay: The Life of Francisco Tárrega

An audio essay on the life of the great guitarist and composer Francisco Tárrega. All music is performed and recorded by me (save for the final piece).

I began by writing the essay based on previous knowledge and immediate research. After I wrote it, I recorded my recitation of the writing. I then recorded various Tárrega pieces and put them in the appropriate contextual spots. I added some sound effects and finished the project.
